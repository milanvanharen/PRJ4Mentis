About Mentis

In a world where people try to hide their mental health problems, we believe that offering trust will be liberating because being mentally satisfied is more important than caring unnecessary weight and a clouded mind.

Health

Health is what keeps people on this earth. However, we think that not only physical health is important. Mental health is equally important and should be taken care of just as much. In times, the two corrolate. How can we be physically happy if we're not mentally happy?

Trust

Trusting someone enough to share your story is very difficult and often a sensitive subject. We at Mentis offer a place with pure trust. Everything said is in confidence and can be done anonymous. Mentis hopes to give people this extra boost to open up.

Safety

Opening up about mental health can be very difficult, especially if one is not in a safe space. Mentis hopes to open a safe place where there is no judging or calling out and you can share your story in a safe environment.

Positiveness

Bad attitude and bad vibes will never help when expressing an opinion. We believe that the glass is always half full and will fully implement this in any advice we give. There are always bad days, but they don't have to be consecutive.
